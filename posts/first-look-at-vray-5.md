## Key Features Overview

### Material Editor Improvements

V-Ray 5 brought a complete overhaul of the material system with new presets, making it faster to create realistic materials. The new Coat and Sheen options give you more control over surface appearance.

### V-Ray Frame Buffer

The new VFB includes powerful new features for reviewing and adjusting your renders, with a more intuitive interface for color correction and channel management.

### Light Mix

One of the most game-changing features — the new Light Mix allows you to adjust the intensity and color of lights in your scene AFTER the render is complete. This gives you incredible flexibility in post-production.

### Layered Compositing

New layered approach to compositing directly within V-Ray, allowing for more complex and non-destructive workflows.
