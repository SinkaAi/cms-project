# Why Build From Scratch?

Why not just use WordPress? Or Webflow? Or one of the many other CMS options out there?

**Because learning is the point.**

When you build something yourself:
- You understand how it works
- You can customize anything
- You own the code
- You can make it do exactly what you want

This project isn't just about having a website. It's about understanding how websites work. And maybe — just maybe — ending up with something pretty cool along the way.

Let's see where this goes. 🌱
