## Introduction

One of the cleanest and most common ways of presenting a product is the good old studio lighting. The reason why many product renders choose this approach is due to the fact that it isolates the product. This isolation in turn makes it so that nothing is taking away the attention from the hero model.

So basically the basic studio lighting uses **3 lights** that act as:
- **Main Light** — The primary illumination source
- **Fill Light** — Softens shadows created by the main light
- **Backdrop Light** — Illuminates the background and separates subject from background

But even though it's really simple on paper, making your renders look good does have its quirks. This video tutorial covers everything you need to know to end up with a very good looking final result.
