I spent my first year learning 3DS Max the hard way. Wrong settings, wasted render hours, models that looked nothing like the reference. I figured it out eventually — but it took longer than it should have.

That's exactly why this site exists.

**I'm Denis Keman** — Autodesk-certified 3DS Max professional, in-house and online training instructor, and a freelancer who's spent years doing real production work with V-Ray, Marvelous Designer, and a stack of other tools. Every post here comes from actual experience on actual projects. Not recycled tutorials. Not generic settings copied from forums. The stuff that actually works.

**What you'll find here:**
- Step-by-step guides for building models in 3DS Max
- V-Ray settings broken down so you actually understand why they work
- Marvelous Designer workflows for realistic fabric and clothing
- Tips from 10+ years of freelancing — including the mistakes
- ZBrush and 3D Coat techniques for cleaning up and detailing your work

Whether you're just starting out or looking to sharpen specific skills, there's something here for you.

Take a look around. Pick a tutorial that matches where you are. And if something helps — let me know. That's the feedback that keeps this going.
